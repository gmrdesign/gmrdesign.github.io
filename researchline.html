RESEARCH LINE

Collaborative system for better information sharing and storage in e-mentoring

This is an internship project in Elsevier. Based on MendeleyGroup, Research Line is a visualization of e-mentoring process for improving the efficiency and effectiveness of information sharing and storage between professor and student

Output: Introduction video, Prototype, Master thesis, User study report

User Research

I interviewed 6 students and 6 professors at the University of Twente to find out the pain points in mentor-student relationships:

  1. Professors：have difficulty in following student's whole research process
  2. Students：have difficulty in collecting professor's evaluation and sharing research process.

Detailed user study report.

Solution

Based on the user research, I designed this research line.

  1. Help student record and visualize their research process
  2. Help mentors keep a overview of supervised students and follow student's research process efficiently.

Design iteration

Three round design iterations have been conducted

Usability testing feedback

  1. The linear solution is the most favorable

  2. Mentors wish to see the big picture and small milestones.
  3. Deadlines and project plan are the key functions for students.

  4. The privacy issue should be carefully considered.
  5. The application should be connected with other social network.

Overall learnings

  1. Fast prototyping is the most efficient way to find out the best designs.
  2. The living condition and envirionments should be considered.
