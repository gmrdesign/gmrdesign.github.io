Background Introduction

Each time when stores get new products, product titles have to be reviewed by legal officers to avoid legal complaints. However, the reviewing process is manully controled and time-consuming. Because of the amount of new goods, legal officers have difficulty reviewing new titles timely, which led to the massive complaints and economic loss. 。

We designed this online system, turn the reviewing system online, improve the reviewing efficiency and reduce complaints rate.

Pain points in current business

The current business has many

Pain points in current business:

  1. Inefficiency: legal officers must search and review the title manully, which is inefficient.
  2. Delay: legal officers have no direct notifications of the updated titles.
  3. No traceback: title's changes were not recorded and can't be traced.

Solution：title review system

There are three moduls in the system:

  1. Review modul: all the new titles update in time in this page, legal officers can see all new titles and can review them immediatelly.
  2. History record: record all the history updated titles.
  3. Dingding notification
