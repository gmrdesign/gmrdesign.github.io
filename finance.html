Project background

Usually, stores will make financial monthly plan at the start of every month and provide it to financial offers to review and approve.

However, the paper monthly plans of different stores are unconsistent, messy and easy to lost, which makes the reviewing and approving work difficult and time-consuming. Based on those problems, we designed an online system for stores to make monthly plan and for financial officiers to make onling reviewing.

Pain points in current business

Pain points in current business:

  1. Calculation mistakes：manually calculated plans cause multiple mistakes.
  2. Inconsistent：different stores make different monthly plan, which are inconsistent
  3. Time-consuming：no real-time notifications of the review results make it time-consuming

Solution

Online monthly plan review system

  1. Stores：make their monthly financial plan online and receive the review results.
  2. Finance officer：review and approve the monthly plan provided by stores.
