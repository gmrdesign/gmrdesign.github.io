Fab labs and STEM education

Parnets want their children to access to new technologies and STEM education in an early age


Fab Lab is a small-scale workshop offering digital fabrication, which is typically equipped with an array of flexible computer-controlled tools, like 3D printer and laser cutters.

Target user and pain points

Our target users are families with children aged 7 to 9

Here are the summary of the user interviews.

Fab labs can help children to access to STEM education in a fun way, however

  1. Outside fab lab: 80% families never heard of fab labs.
  2. Insdie fab lab: 70% people don't know what they can do in fab labs。

Our solution

We designed this online platform fabby which provide children and parents fun field trips to fab labs. There are three steps in the service:

  1. Online ordering: children and parents choose and order an activity on Fabby.eu，and reserve a time in nearby fab labs.
  2. In Fab lab: parents bring their children to the reserved fab lab, made their fun project under the instruction of fab lab staff.
  3. Online sharing: children post their projects online and share in the online community.
